# The Persona Wallet App

⚠️ This guide is written for individuals who download the Persona Wallet App from Apple’s App Store or Google’s Play Store.

# Overview[](#overview)

## What does the Persona Wallet App do?[](#what-does-the-persona-wallet-app-do)

The Persona Wallet App allows end-user to manage the verified PII stored on their [Reusable Persona](./4Yl6a7VEH2ScPfAHDif0Ts.md).

With the Persona Wallet App, you can:

-   Create a Persona
-   Delete a Persona
-   Add forms of government-issued ID like a drivers license, Passport, Visa, state ID, non-citizen travel document, etc
-   Add a selfie

# Frequently Asked Questions[](#frequently-asked-questions)

## Where can I download the Persona App?[](#where-can-i-download-the-persona-app)

You can download the Persona Wallet app on either [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/persona-identities/id6478614002) and [Google’s Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.withpersona.app.reusablepersonas "Google Play Store: The Persona Wallet App").

## How can I use the Persona Wallet App?[](#how-can-i-use-the-persona-wallet-app)

In addition to managing your Persona, adding data to the Persona Wallet App can speed up your login process across services that utilize Reusable Personas.
