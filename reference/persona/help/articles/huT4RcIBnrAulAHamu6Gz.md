# What is Persona?

Persona provides a powerful, secure platform to help organizations collect, verify, store, and analyze the identity of any individual in the world.

In simple terms, Persona is a comprehensive identity management platform.

Part of Persona's capabilities include:

-   Collection, verification, enrichment, and analysis of user information
-   Enablement of decision-making based on user information analysis
-   Integration of third-party data for additional insights
-   Evaluatation of behavioral risk signals and automates decisions using customizable workflows
