# Quick Start: Set up your first identity verification flow

## Overview[](#overview)

Welcome to Persona — we are so excited to have you on board!

This guide takes you through the steps to set up your first identity verification flow. It covers:

-   Key concepts in Persona to learn first
-   How to try out your Inquiry flow
-   Customizing the logic and look-and-feel of your flow
-   The different integration methods you can use to deploy your flow
-   Common FAQs

## Checklist[](#checklist)

### Learn general Persona concepts[](#learn-general-persona-concepts)

1.  Learn key concepts to gain an understanding of Persona.
    -   [Key concepts: Inquiry, Verification, and other terms](./14hgKH7lZo9b6ja7tRJobW.md)

### Try out Persona[](#try-out-persona)

1.  Add a solution in your Sandbox environment.
    -   [Add a solution](./67J7FurQtIgwxkWWvUropu.md)
2.  Create a first Inquiry to understand what it's like to use Persona as an end user.
    -   [Tutorial: Create a test Inquiry](./1snRc3bFMYhykFN6vhLith.md)
3.  View the result of the Inquiry in the Dashboard. See details including what information was collected from you, and whether each Verification check passed or failed.
    -   [Inquiry details in the Dashboard](./nqBDRxxIjiIvnOwsCpri6.md)
    -   [Tutorial: Understand Verification results for an Inquiry](./xrnJpm5Q18CSN63SQFdsi.md)

### Customize your Inquiry flow[](#customize-your-inquiry-flow)

1.  Customize your Inquiry flow.
    -   [Configuring your Inquiry Template](./ETA0GIS8K60DSoiFRpA9z.md)
    -   Note that changes you make to your Inquiry Templates in Sandbox are reflected in Production, and vice versa.
2.  Preview the Inquiry flow you've customized.
    -   [Preview an Inquiry](./4Bc5EGnIOwAiXZaijGISsx.md)

### Integrate your Inquiry flow into your app[](#integrate-your-inquiry-flow-into-your-app)

1.  Decide on and implement the integration method that works best for you. [Choosing your implementation method](./KLR2XBp2amPOIuDoqg1QY.md) walks you through these options:
    -   [Embedded](./Jx6UeQzh5tVSkoynev7bt.md) - Seamlessly verify individuals within your website.
    -   [Hosted](./4pZBZYAFLkKMyXycGeAMV2.md) - Send users a link to your flow.
    -   [Mobile SDK](./ac6Uc0xr5PsLoqKB0CHPr.md) - Implement Persona directly within your native Android or iOS app.

### Other steps[](#other-steps)

1.  Add additional team members to your organization’s Dashboard when you are ready.
    -   [Managing users and user permissions](./5isJzuUdTmFWiLehSDugUI.md)
