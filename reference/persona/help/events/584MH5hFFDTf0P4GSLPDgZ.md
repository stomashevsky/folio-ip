# Build design systems that stick

![design systems meetup](../images/Image_from_Persona_Mail_d4daa2c6c595.png)

Calling all web developers, JavaScript enthusiasts, and design systems pros!

-   Join the San Francisco dev community on the night of 7th February for a frontend meetup, exploring best practice workflows for design systems teams.
    
-   From building, testing and documenting your components, to measuring your DS’s performance, you’ll learn how to work more effectively with your team to ship fantastic frontend applications. There’ll be presentations by Joseph Le (Persona), Sertac Olgunsoylu (Omlet), and Kyle Gach (Chromatic / Storybook core maintainer).
    
-   Plus, expect plenty of time for hanging out with other like-minded devs and meeting new people, free swag and refreshments.
    
-   Tickets are free but limited, so book now to avoid disappointment!
    

Hosted by Chromatic, Storybook, Omlet, and Persona.
