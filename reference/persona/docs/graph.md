# Graph

Supercharge your fraud investigations with Graph, Persona’s link analysis tool. Querying against your data in Graph allows you to enrich your understanding of identity-related entities and their relationships — letting you catch fraudsters faster, and at scale.

## Why Graph?

Get the power of link analysis to uncover bad actors without painful data preparation. Bring together data sources, both from inside and outside of Persona, inside of Graph, and see suspicious clusters automatically surfaced.  Use our flexible data model to reflect both the scale and complexity of your customer base without custom engineering work. Easily find and save key clusters for your team to monitor, and overcome poor quality data with entity resolution.
