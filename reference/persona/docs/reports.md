# Overview

Reports allow you to enrich your understanding of identity-related entities using key identifiers like email addresses, phone numbers, individuals, businesses, etc. Persona supports many Report types, including:

-   Address Lookup
-   Adverse Media
-   Business Adverse Media
-   Business Watchlist
-   Email Risk
-   Phone Risk
-   Social Media Lookup
-   Watchlist
