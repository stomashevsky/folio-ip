# Solution demo: Workflows to enforce age thresholds and gather parental consent

Explore how you can create age verification workflows to comply with regulations while keeping users happy.

Regulations like the Online Safety Act (UK) and the Digital Services Act (EU) call for stricter age-related safeguards. In the US, KOSPA recently passed in the Senate, and 19 states have passed online age verification laws that have either already gone into effect, or will be in effect by July 2025. As a result, businesses across social media, gaming, and entertainment may soon need to rethink their age verification and privacy controls.

Watch this discussion and product demo to learn how to balance compliance and conversion as the age verification landscape continues to shift.
