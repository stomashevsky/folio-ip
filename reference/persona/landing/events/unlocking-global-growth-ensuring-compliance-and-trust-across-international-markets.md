# Unlocking global growth: Ensuring compliance and trust across international markets

Industry experts Luke Charters, Head of Compliance at Remote, and Jeff Sakasegawa, Trust & Safety Architect at Persona, discuss how global companies build user onboarding programs with trust & safety in mind.

In this on-demand webinar, we review valuable insights into combating the evolving sophistication of fraudulent know your business (KYB) attempts, the importance of a global team, and navigating the landscape of region-specific fraud detection methods alongside the compliance challenges posed by dispersed companies and teams.

---

Watch today to hear about:

-   Challenges of building and running a global fraud strategy given an increasingly mobile workforce.
-   How shared intelligence can help fraud and compliance at scale.
-   Considerations for fighting fraud globally while maintaining flexibility in your onboarding processes.
