# Trust & Safety in the Age of ChatGPT and AI

As AI technologies become commodities, they become convenient mass weapons for fraudsters to generate deepfakes, voicefakes, and synthetic IDs. But rather than fighting AI with a single silver bullet, companies should take a layered approach and constantly adapt their strategy to prevent this kind of fraud from overwhelming their system. In this webinar, learn actionable tips from trust and safety leads from Persona and Rently on how to scale trust and safety processes against an ever-rising tide of AI-generated content.

---

Watch this webinar to learn:

-   What the “scariest thing in fraud” is for Sahil — and how to get ahead of it.
-   How to take a layered approach to fighting AI-powered fraud.
-   Which of your existing tools and processes may help you combat emerging threats.
