## How it works

Persona offers a variety of building blocks to solve any identity challenge. We recommend creating an account and applying from the dashboard once familiar with the platform.

Step 1

### Create a sandbox account

Explore Persona's full suite of building blocks and guide yourself through the integration process using Sandbox credentials.

Step 2

### Choose a cohort and apply

When you're ready, head over to the billing page to select the plan or program that best fits your needs.

Step 3

### Set up production

Once accepted, switch to production credentials to begin using Persona. We'll also invite key team members to the Personally Slack community.

Frequently asked questions

Is this really free?

Toggle description visibility

Yes! You get up to 12 months of free access to Persona's core tools, including 500 free verifications per month.

What happens after 12 months?

Toggle description visibility

After 12 months, your account will be automatically deactivated. To avoid this, you can transition to a paid plan. We'll work with you to ensure a smooth transition and continue supporting you as you scale.

Do I need a credit card to apply?

Toggle description visibility

A credit card is required to apply or get started, but you'll only be charged if you exceed your monthly quota.

What if I'm already using Persona?

Toggle description visibility

Current Persona customers aren't eligible for the Startup Program, but we want to make sure we support you in whatever way we can. Contact our team at [startups@withpersona.com](mailto:startups@withpersona.com) to discuss your options.

Can international startups apply?

Toggle description visibility

Yes! We welcome applications from startups worldwide. Some program benefits like in-person events may be limited to certain geographic regions.

Can I refer another startup?

Toggle description visibility

Absolutely! We love referrals from our community. Referred startups get priority review, and successful referrers get exclusive perks and recognition.

What type of product support do I get in the Startup Program?

Toggle description visibility

The Startup Program offers you self-serve digital resources for implementation and ongoing support. Unfortunately, Persona is not able to offer guaranteed support from our Customer Support team for businesses on the Persona Startup Program.  
  
As part of the Startup Program, you'll have complete access to a wealth of digital resources including the [Help Center](https://help.withpersona.com/), [Developer Docs](../docs/index.md), [Persona Academy](https://academy.withpersona.com/trainings), and [Copilot](https://help.withpersona.com/articles/7MVBDz7vujOdASqRwUl5VY/).
