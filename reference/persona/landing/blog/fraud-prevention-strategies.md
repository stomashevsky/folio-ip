Key takeaways

Fraud prevention is the use of policies and procedures to counteract fraud and other financial crimes.

There is no “secret sauce” when it comes to adequate fraud protection, but Know Your Customer (KYC) processes are a good start.

For forward-thinking businesses, fraud prevention can pay for itself.
