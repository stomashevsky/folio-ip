# Guide to Know Your Business (KYB) in banking

A strong Know Your Business (KYB) program is the best way for banks and financial institutions to protect against fraud and other financial crimes.

Key takeaways

Shell companies were the impetus for Know Your Business (KYB) regulations, which aim to stay ahead of ever-evolving money launderers and fraudsters.

KYB compliance protects against money laundering risk and fraud by ensuring that ownership and control are fully understood prior to transactions taking place at banks and a growing category of financial institutions.

KYB in banking is typically followed by a Know Your Customer (KYC) review on the individuals who have been identified.
