Key takeaways

If you want to catch business impersonation during the KYB process, you need to spend time thinking about the specifics of your business.  

It’s important to consider customer expectations when developing your KYB strategy, so you don’t lose customers to competitors. 

There are four tips you can use to build a stronger KYB process: understand your user behavior, collect a variety of data points, monitor continuously, and implement feedback loops.
