# Generative AI in the world of trust and safety

LLMs and other types of generative AI have the potential to destroy customer trust in your marketplace or platform. Learn more about the risks and solutions.

Key takeaways

Generative AI is artificial intelligence capable of creating brand new text, images, video, and audio from scratch.

Generative AI empowers bad actors to engage in a variety of different types of fraud that can damage user trust in your platform, including AI promo abuse, AI phishing attacks, and AI identity fraud.

Protecting your business requires a firm understanding of your user journey and the unique risks your platform is exposed to at each key juncture.
