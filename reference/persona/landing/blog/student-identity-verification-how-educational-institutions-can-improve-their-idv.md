Key takeaways

The goal of student identity verification is to ensure that the person who registers for a class, course, certificate, or degree — and who will receive the credential or credit associated with it — is the same person who actually _completes_ the material.

Student IDV is important because it helps build trust in the online education industry. Institutions and platforms want to know that they are not being defrauded by the learners using their platform, and employers want to know that an applicant’s educational credentials are not fraudulent.
