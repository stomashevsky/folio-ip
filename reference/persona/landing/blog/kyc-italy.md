# KYC in Italy: How businesses can stay compliant

Learn about the KYC regulatory process in Italy and see how businesses can evolve and grow while maintaining compliance standards.

Key takeaways

Italy has long been proactive about fighting financial crimes and was among the first countries to criminalize money laundering in 1978.

Mandatory beneficial ownership reporting for certain companies in Italy became official in October 2023.

Failure to comply with financial regulations in Italy can range from fines to prison terms.
