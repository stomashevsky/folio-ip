Key takeaways

Identity is not one-size-fits-all. It's essential to take a holistic approach to identity verification to gain confidence that the person behind the screen is who they say they are.

Adopting a holistic approach to identity involves taking multiple signals into account, including active, passive, and behavioral signals.

Progressive risk segmentation is a strategy that helps you balance risk and conversion by triggering automatic step-up verifications based on the information collected and observed during an interaction.
