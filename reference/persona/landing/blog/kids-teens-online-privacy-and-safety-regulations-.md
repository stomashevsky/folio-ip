# Kids’ online privacy and safety: Navigating data collection and protection regulations for minors

Learn more about the online privacy and safety regulations your business must comply with if you collect and process data from children and teenagers.

Key takeaways

Regulators worldwide have enacted laws to limit the collection and processing of minors’ data.

Over the past year, dozens of new bills have been introduced to better protect the privacy of minors online.

To comply with data processing and collection regulations and remain adaptable to new ones on the horizon, businesses should implement user-friendly and flexible age verification systems.
