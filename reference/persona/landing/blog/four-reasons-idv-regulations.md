Key takeaways

The identity space has become more complicated in recent years, increasing the pressure on legal, identity, and fraud professionals to get compliance right.

This is in part because more industries are facing IDV regulations and more businesses are operating internationally. 

Increased levels of fraud, thanks to generative AI, and an increased focus on privacy have only complicated things further.

Businesses trying to manage should focus on building a strategy that is attuned to their current needs while being flexible enough to meet future needs — whatever they may be.
