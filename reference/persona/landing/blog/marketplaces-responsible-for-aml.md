# Are marketplaces responsible for AML?

Learn who’s responsible for complying with AML regulations, and what your marketplace can do to prevent fraud.

![Shana Vu](../images/Shana_Vu-headshot_aab6d6d53068.png)

Shana Vu

Key takeaways

Payment processors — not marketplaces themselves — have to comply with AML laws. 

However, marketplaces are responsible for complying with their payment processor’s rules. 

Marketplaces need to adopt a variety of fraud prevention systems and tools, from KYC to IDV.
