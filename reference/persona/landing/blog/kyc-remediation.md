Key takeaways

KYC remediation involves updating customer documents and other information collected through the KYC process.

Remediation can help ensure you're complying with ever-changing regulations and accurately assessing risk.

The KYC remediation process includes identifying impacted data, making data updates, and deleting data when necessary.
