Key takeaways

Know Your Transaction (KYT) processes enhance AML programs by using sophisticated rulesets to closely monitor customer transaction data for suspicious activity.

KYT involves the continuous monitoring, analysis, and tracking of client transactions. There are three basic steps: data analysis, risk assessment, and reporting.

Upfront technology investments can be greatly offset by the reduction in risk and potential avoidance of costly regulatory fines.
