Key takeaways

Identity proofing is the process of gathering the information needed to establish someone’s identity. 

Companies across industries use identity proofing to help prevent fraud and ensure regulatory compliance.  

The most effective way to conduct identity proofing is to incorporate it into a robust identity verification framework.
