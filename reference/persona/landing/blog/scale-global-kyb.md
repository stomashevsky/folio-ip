# How to create scalable and compliant international KYB processes

Industry experts discuss international KYB and debunk common myths while sharing how to build a scalable global KYB process.

Key takeaways

Copying and pasting your current KYB process when entering a new country could lead to trouble down the road. 

You have to account for regional regulations, data consistency and availability, and local consumer expectations. 

Be adaptable and create international KYB processes based on your company’s goals and risk tolerance.
