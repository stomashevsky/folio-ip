# RegTech: Both a necessity and a differentiator

RegTech can transform compliance from simply a requirement to a point of differentiation. See how.

Key takeaways

Compliance work, especially watchlist screenings, are rife with false positives. Improvements in the field, such as Persona’s Watchlist Portrait Match, will drastically reduce the repetitive operations that prevent teams from substantive investigations.

It’s not build versus buy — realistically, it’s build _and_ buy. Financial institutions have a myriad of proprietary payment and compliance tech which they have purposefully built. Buying a platform that can integrate with and automate across this tech should be the goal for compliance teams looking to combat fraud and promote growth.

Configurability and adaptability will become table stakes for compliance technology as more regulations and requirements enter the market, especially as they have considerations by state, country, or region.
