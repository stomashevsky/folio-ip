# Pouring responsibly: age verification for online alcohol retailers

Online alcohol sellers need to reliably verify customers' ages during checkout and delivery. This guide is here to help.

Key takeaways

Online alcohol retailers need to comply with age verification regulations, but those can  vary by state.

Underage consumers are often able to circumvent traditional measures designed to prevent them from purchasing alcohol, opening up serious risk for retailers who could have their licenses suspended or revoked.  

Luckily, new age verification solutions are helping online alcohol retailers adopt more stringent compliance flows without overburdening users.
