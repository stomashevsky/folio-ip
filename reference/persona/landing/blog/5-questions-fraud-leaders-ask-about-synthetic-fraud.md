Key takeaways

Consider taking into account data such as a user's social media information, how many accounts they've opened, and device characteristics.

Biometrics can introduce less friction, but you may not want to solely rely on this verification method.

While generative AI may help fraud grow increasingly sophisticated, there are also new technologies being developed to counter these attacks.
