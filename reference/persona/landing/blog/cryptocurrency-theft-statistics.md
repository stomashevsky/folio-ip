Key takeaways

Fraudsters used a variety of techniques to steal $3.8 billion in cryptocurrency in 2022 alone.

There are several risks inherent to crypto platforms that make it vulnerable to cyber crime, including digital wallet vulnerabilities, decentralized crypto exchanges, and few government regulations.

Know Your Customer (KYC) processes can help deter cryptocurrency theft.
