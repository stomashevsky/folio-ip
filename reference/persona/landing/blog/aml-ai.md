Key takeaways

Artificial intelligence (AI) is going to revolutionize how companies approach anti-money laundering (AML).

Natural language processing, network analysis, and predictive analysis tools are well matched to address the requirements that regulators demand of financial institutions.

AI is speedy, learns with experience, and is easily adaptable, making it a cost-effective solution for companies with high employee overhead.
