Key takeaways

Criminals often use a process known as structuring or smurfing to make their dirty money appear legitimate so they can spend it with less risk of raising suspicion.

The AML process typically consists of multiple strategies, including KYC, CDD, customer screening, transaction monitoring, suspicious activity reporting, and more.
