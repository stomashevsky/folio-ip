# Identity challenges in the travel industry: How hospitality businesses can fight fraud

Identity fraud in the travel industry has become increasingly common. Here are some common identity challenges and potential solutions businesses need to know about.

Key takeaways

Account takeover, loyalty fraud, and promo abuse continue to plague the travel and hospitality space as bad actors use stolen information and doctored documents to wreak havoc on legitimate users and businesses. 

Implementing identity verification (IDV), reverification, multi-factor authentication, and other measures can help you protect your business when these types of attacks and others occur — and even prevent them from happening.
