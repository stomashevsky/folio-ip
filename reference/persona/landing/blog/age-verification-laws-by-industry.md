# Understanding industry-specific age verification regulations

Age verification laws vary significantly from industry to industry. Learn about requirements for social media, adult entertainment, retail, and other industries.

Key takeaways

Age verification bills and regulations are moving in a more nuanced direction, which will shape user experiences for individuals of all ages.

Around the world, industry-specific age verification regulations have been passed affecting social media, adult content, and age-restricted goods such as alcohol and tobacco.

With no global consensus on the exact ages by which users can access specific age-restricted experiences and goods, a dynamic, configurable age verification system can help companies comply with a patchwork of regulations and provide better experiences at scale.
