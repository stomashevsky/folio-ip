# Why does KYC matter for fintech companies?

Whether you offer a lending product or investing service, if you're a fintech company, you need to comply with KYC regulations.

Key takeaways

KYC is the process of verifying each customer's identity.

The Bank Secrecy Act requires all financial institutions to comply with KYC regulations.

While KYC can add friction, you can encourage conversions by implementing shorter, multi-step forms, easy session transfers between devices, and progressive risk segmentation during the identity verification process.
