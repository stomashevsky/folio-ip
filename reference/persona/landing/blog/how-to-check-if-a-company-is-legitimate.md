Key takeaways

Working with fraudulent or illegitimate companies costs you time and money, which is why it’s crucial to verify the legitimacy of every business you associate with. 

There are a few ways to determine a company’s legitimacy, including verifying their registrations and reviewing their online activity.

For most businesses, a Know Your Business (KYB) strategy will be the most comprehensive, reliable solution.
