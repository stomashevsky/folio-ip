# INFORM Consumers Act: Definition and FAQs

The [**INFORM Consumers Act**](../blog/what-is-the-inform-consumers-act.md) is a law that requires online marketplaces operating in the United States to implement specific measures designed to reduce the sale of stolen and counterfeit goods. These measures may also be effective at mitigating other types of [marketplace fraud](../blog/what-is-marketplace-fraud-and-how-do-companies-stop-it.md).
