# What is Politically Exposed Person (PEP) Definition

A [politically exposed person (PEP)](../blog/what-is-a-politically-exposed-person-pep.md) is someone in a public, high-ranking position of power and influence. This status puts them at a higher risk of being involved in or linked to financial crimes like corruption, bribery, or [money laundering](../blog/what-is-anti-money-laundering-aml-and-why-is-it-important.md).
