# Know Your Customer (KYC) | Definition & FAQs

[Know Your Customer (KYC)](../blog/know-your-customer-kyc.md), also known as [customer due diligence](../blog/know-your-customer-kyc-vs-customer-due-diligence-cdd-whats-the-difference.md) or know your client, is the process of ​​verifying current or prospective customers’ identities and assessing the potential risks of doing business with them.
