# Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) | Definition & FAQs

An ultimate beneficial owner (UBO) is the (or one of the) persons or entities that are the ultimate beneficiaries of a company. Certain financial and other organizations, including banks, currency exchange offices, and insurers, are subject to mandatory disclosures of UBOs if doing business with any party.
