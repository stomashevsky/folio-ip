# What is a False Negative? | Definition & FAQ

In the context of [identity verification](../index.md), a false negative refers to a situation in which an individual isn’t able to get verified even though they’re actually who they say they are. In other words, keeping a legitimate user out.

In the context of fraud prevention, a false negative refers to incorrectly approving a bad actor or fraudulent transaction. In other words, allowing fraud through.
